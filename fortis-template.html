New Year - New Projects

As we enter an exciting new year, the outlook for 2018 is unbelievable. With projects ongoing throughout Ontario, our Fortis team continues to strive to excel and grow with our clients and their construction needs. From everyone at Fortis, we say thank you and here's to an amazing new year.

This is how we continue to #BUILDSTRONG.

Walmart - London, Cambridge & Sault Ste. Marie, ON

Walmart Canada awarded Fortis Group three major renovation projects. Our teams are currently renovating stores in London, Cambridge and Sault Ste. Marie by providing a fresh, new look to each location. All three stores continue to operate during regular store hours as our crews work diligently to complete these renovations for the retail giant.

Upper Canada Mall - Newmarket, ON

When Upper Canada Mall called upon Fortis Group to renovate two of the mall entrances, our team walked through the doors and began the process during the busiest shopping season of the year. Within days, the former facade was removed and work started on the new modern design to greet shoppers as they entered the mall.

Guelph Nissan - Guelph, ON

Fortis Group along with Architectural Design Associates Inc (ADA) will soon begin working on the new Nissan Retail Environment Design Initiative (NREDI 2.0) renovation project at Guelph Nissan for the Yates Automotive Group.

The rollout of the NREDI 2.0 dealership in Guelph signals the next generation of this new standard for dealerships under the Nissan brand.

Erin Mills Town Centre - Mississauga, ON

When Cushman & Wakefield contracted Fortis Group to prepare several stores within the Erin Mills Town Centre in Mississauga, our team went to work right away.

An entire section of the mall required extensive work to make room for a group of new tenants in a very short period of time. The new tenants included Ortho World, Ten Thousand Villages, Clair De Lune, Smart Wheel and Augie Doggy to name a few.
